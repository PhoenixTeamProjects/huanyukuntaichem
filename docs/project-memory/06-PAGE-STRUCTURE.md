# 06 - Page Structure

## Routes

- `/[locale]`
- `/[locale]/products`
- `/[locale]/products/category/[slug]`
- `/[locale]/products/[slug]`
- `/[locale]/news`
- `/[locale]/news/[slug]`
- `/[locale]/applications`
- `/[locale]/service`
- `/[locale]/about`
- `/[locale]/contact`

## Product Pages

Products and product categories must include a category menu with parent/child product aggregation. The first version includes the route and utility foundation; Directus category depth and expandable mobile behavior must be completed after real categories are confirmed.

## Inquiry Entrances

Inquiry forms are present on home, product detail, news detail, and contact in the initial skeleton. Applications, service, and about should receive contextual CTA sections when production content is added.
